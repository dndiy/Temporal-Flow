## What is Temporal Flow?

Temporal Flow is a decentralized content sharing platform built for creators. It's an all-in-one hub for hosting and sharing creative content in a central location while making it easy to discover and connect with other creators.

Unlike traditional platforms that control your content on centralized servers, Temporal Flow gives you full ownership of your work with the benefits of a connected community.

## Get Started in Minutes

Setting up your Temporal Flow site is straightforward:

1. **Create your site** - Deploy your own Temporal Flow instance
2. **Customize your space** - Adjust the design to match your style
3. **Add your content** - Start publishing with simple markdown files
4. **Connect with others** - Build your creative network

## Why Choose Temporal Flow?

Temporal Flow combines the freedom of self-hosting with the connectivity of social platforms. You maintain complete ownership of your content while benefiting from a network of connected creators.

No algorithms controlling who sees your content. No platforms taking a cut of your earnings. Just a simple, powerful way to share your creativity with the world.
