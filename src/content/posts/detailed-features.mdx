      <h4 className="font-bold mb-2">Content Federation</h4>
      <p className="text-sm text-gray-700 dark:text-neutral-300">

      <p className="text-sm text-gray-700 dark:text-neutral-300">

      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div>
      <h4 className="font-bold mb-2">Content Packages</h4>
      <p className="text-sm text-gray-700 dark:text-neutral-300">
        Export and import content directly between sites. Share specific articles or collections with fellow creators while maintaining proper attribution and source links.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
  
  <h4 className="font-bold text-lg mt-6 mb-3 text-gray-700 dark:text-neutral-400">How Friend Sharing Works</h4>
  
  <p className="text-gray-700 dark:text-neutral-300 mb-4">
    Behind the scenes, the system works through special files that contain information about your blog and recent posts:
  </p>
  
  <ol className="list-decimal pl-5 space-y-2 text-gray-700 dark:text-neutral-300">
    <li>Each Temporal Flow site generates a <code>friend-content.json</code> file with metadata about recent posts</li>
    <li>When you add a friend, your site fetches their content file to retrieve post information</li>
    <li>Content is synchronized whenever you manually refresh from the admin panel</li>
    <li>Friend connections are stored as markdown files in your repository</li>
    <li>CORS headers enable cross-domain content sharing</li>
  </ol>
